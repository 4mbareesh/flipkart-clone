WHAT IS SHOPSY?


Shopsy by Flipkart is a brand new 0% commission platform for sellers. The platform enables them to enhance
their reach to approximately 200-500 million budget-friendly customers. With Shopsy, sellers can cater to this
category of consumers with Resellers who the consumers trust and generate profit through a trust-based selling platform.
Resellers form an interim layer between seller and customers.

Shopsy creates a layer of distribution between e-commerce sellers and consumers through Resellers. By leveraging a platform to
create an earning, Shopsy permits sellers to target the right audience who would look out for affordable products.
